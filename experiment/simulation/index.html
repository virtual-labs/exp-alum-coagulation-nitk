Alum Coagulation

Objective:

To determine the optimum Alum dosage for water or waste water treatment by Jar test.


Apparatus used:

Nephelometer, Nephelometer tube, Pipette, Beaker, Stirrer and Measuring cylinder etc.


Solutions used Water samples, Alum solution (Al2(SO4)318H2O) Description Coagulation is the process of addition of a chemical to de-stabilize a stabilized charged particle.

Flocculation is a slow mixing technique which promotes agglomeration and helps the particles to settle down.
